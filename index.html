Bonsai Money Tree (Crassula ovate)

Care

The Bonsai Money Tree is considered by many to be a symbol of good luck and prosperity for those who own one.

Keep watering to a minimum to encourage small leaves. The more water the plant is given, the larger and fleshier the leaves become. Allow the soil to dry out before watering during the winter months.

Castiron Plant (Aspidistra elatior)

Care

This plant is “mostly for foliage,” meaning if you're looking to add a lush, dark green plant to your space, this one is for you. It does well in medium to low light and is tolerant of neglect, so it's fine if you forget to water it once in a while.

Water once a week or every ten days.

Wisteria Bonsai Tree
$60

Wisteria Bonsai Tree (Wisteria sp.)

Care

Wisterias trees will not bloom until they are at least 10-years-old. The wisteria seed pods and seed are highly toxic if ingested: Exercise care around children and pets.

The wisteria tree needs a great amount of water when it is growing, especially just prior to and during its blooming cycle. Ideally, the pot should be set in a tray of water.
